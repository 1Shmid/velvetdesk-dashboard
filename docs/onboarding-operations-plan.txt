## üëî OPERATOR HANDOFF STRATEGY

### When to Transfer to Human

**Triggers:**
1. Customer explicitly asks: "—Ö–æ—á—É –º–µ–Ω–µ–¥–∂–µ—Ä–∞", "real person"
2. AI confidence < 70% (uncertain answer)
3. Complaint or refund request
4. Complex booking modification

### Transfer Methods

**Method A: Direct Call Transfer (VAPI feature)**
- AI says: "Connecting you to our manager..."
- VAPI transfers to business.contact_phone
- Works 0-10 clients (low volume)

**Method B: Ticket + Callback**
- AI: "Manager will call you back in 10 minutes"
- Create support_tickets record (customer_name, phone, issue)
- Email business owner: "Urgent callback needed"
- Works 10-50 clients

**Method C: Support Dashboard (50+ clients)**
- Dedicated support operator pool
- /dashboard/support-tickets
- SLA: 10 min response time

### Norms & Procedures

**Response Times:**
- Urgent (complaint): 10 min callback
- Standard (question): 30 min
- Low priority: 2 hours

**Business Hours Only:**
- Outside hours: "We're closed, call back Mon-Fri 9-18"
- Emergency line: business owner decides (optional)

**Quality Checks:**
- Random call review (10% sample)
- Customer satisfaction survey after handoff
- AI learns from human operator responses
